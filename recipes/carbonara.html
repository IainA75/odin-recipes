Spaghetti Carbonara

A quick and easy creamy italian pasta dish, made with absolutely no cream!

Ingredients

  • 500g spaghetti
  • 2 tbsps olive oil
  • 8 slices of smoked bacon
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 clove of fresh garlic
  • 1/4 cup dry white wine
  • 4 large eggs, beaten
  • 1/2 cup of grated parmesan
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tbsps chopped fresh parsley
  • 2 tbsps grated parmesan

Method

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to the boil. Cook spaghetti in boling water until cooked (al dente is recommended). Drain the spaghetti and mix with 1 tbsp of olive oil. Set aside for now. ( Hint- keep some of the pasta water incase you need to add more liquid to the dish before serving)
  2. Pan fry the diced bacon over a medium heat ( approx 10 minutes). Drain any excess fat, saving some in the pan for later.
  3. Add 1 tbsp olive oil to remaining bacon fat. Add chopped onion and cook on medium heat until the onion is translucent. Add the pressed garlic close and cook for about 1 minute. Add white wine and cook 1 minute more.
  4. Return cooked bacon to the pan; add cooked spaghetti. Toss to coat and heat through, adding more olive oil if it seems dry or sticks together. Add beaten eggs and cook, tossing constantly with tongs or a large fork, until eggs are barely set. Quickly add 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese and toss again. Season with salt and pepper (remember that bacon and Parmesan are very salty).
  5. Serve warm with chopped parsley sprinkled on top and extra Parmesan cheese at the table.
